About WonderzNet

This index was started in 1996 by a group of students at North Cascadia Technical College
http://www.nctc.edu/~media/index.html

It is part of an independent study on early web communities.

The project focuses on identifying and maintaining a small collection of web resources. Links are added manually and reviewed on a regular basis.

WonderzNet is publicly accessible during the duration of the study.

Reference: https://info.cern.ch/
