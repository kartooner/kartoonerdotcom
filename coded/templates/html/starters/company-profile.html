About us

InGen is a pioneering bioengineering corporation specializing in genetic research and de-extinction technology. Founded in 1975, we've established ourselves as the world's leading authority in cloning and genetic manipulation, pushing the boundaries of what's scientifically possible.

Our mission is to unlock the secrets of prehistoric DNA and bring wonder back to the modern world. Through cutting-edge genetic engineering and ambitious biological attractions, we're creating experiences that will change how humanity views the natural world forever.
