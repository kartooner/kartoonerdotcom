The Kerner Company

Optical Research Lab

Our journey

1975

Founded

Established as an optical effects research facility on Kerner Blvd in San Rafael, Marin County, pioneering new techniques in motion picture photography.

1977

First major project

Delivered groundbreaking optical compositing work, combining miniatures, motion control, and innovative matte techniques.

1985

Expansion

Opened dedicated model shop and expanded optical printer capacity. Team grew to over 200 artists and technicians.

1993

Digital transition

Began integrating digital compositing alongside traditional optical techniques, bridging practical and computer-generated effects.

2006

Today

Continuing our legacy of innovation in visual effects, model making, and practical photography for film and television.
